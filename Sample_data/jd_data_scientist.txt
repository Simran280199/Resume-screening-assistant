Job Title: Data Scientist

About the Role:
We are looking for a Data Scientist to join our analytics team. The ideal
candidate will build machine learning models to solve business problems,
communicate insights to stakeholders, and help deploy models into production.

Required Skills:
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Hands-on experience with machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, XGBoost)
- Solid understanding of statistics and hypothesis testing
- Experience with deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow or PyTorch)
- Experience deploying models to a cloud platform (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau or Power BI)
- Familiarity with MLOps practices (Docker, model monitoring, MLflow)
- Excellent communication skills to present findings to non-technical stakeholders

Preferred Qualifications:
- 3+ years of experience in a data science or applied ML role
- Experience with NLP or time series forecasting
- Master's degree in a quantitative field (Computer Science, Statistics, etc.)

Responsibilities:
- Design, build, and evaluate machine learning models end-to-end
- Partner with engineering to deploy models into production systems
- Communicate findings and recommendations to leadership
- Mentor junior team members on best practices
